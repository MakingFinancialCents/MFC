Making sense of your financial future

       

Program participants will gain an understanding of five basic financial literacy principles.


  • Banking

  • Budgeting

  • College Financing

  • Insurance

  • Crediting





       

The Making Financial Cents program is instructional, educational and interactive by applying three components:


  1. Utilize an instructor/student friendly presentation that covers the five major categories of concern and educates as to why financial literacy is important.

  2. Lesson Plan/Workbook will have supplemental information, activities, and materials you can use to engage program participants—examples in the workbook will be relatable to Austinites.

  3. An assessment that measures the participants’ aptitude on the five financial topics along with an exit survey that informs the program staff on the needs of the community.
